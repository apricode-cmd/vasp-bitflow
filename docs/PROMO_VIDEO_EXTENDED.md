## üéØ Voiceover Production Specs

### Recording Requirements:
- **Format:** WAV (uncompressed) or FLAC
- **Sample Rate:** 48kHz minimum (96kHz preferred)
- **Bit Depth:** 24-bit minimum
- **Channels:** Mono (for voiceover)
- **Signal-to-Noise Ratio:** >60dB
- **Room Tone:** Record 10s at start/end for editing

### Post-Production:
- **Normalize:** -3dB to -6dB peak level
- **Compression:** Light (2:1 to 3:1 ratio)
- **EQ:** High-pass filter at 80Hz, boost presence at 3-5kHz
- **De-essing:** If needed for sibilants
- **Noise Reduction:** Light (preserve natural quality)
- **Export:** AAC 320kbps for video

### Voice Talent Guidelines:
- **Gender:** Male or female (client preference)
- **Age Range:** 30-50 (mature, trustworthy voice)
- **Accent:** Neutral English
  - **American:** General American (broadcast standard)
  - **British:** Received Pronunciation (BBC English)
  - **International:** Clear, neutral, easy to understand
- **Experience:** Corporate/commercial voiceover experience required
- **Demo Reel:** Must include fintech/technology samples

### Budget Estimates:
- **Professional Studio VO:** $400-800
- **Freelance (experienced):** $200-400
- **Voice.com / Fiverr (quality):** $100-250
- **AI Voice (ElevenLabs):** $50-100 (not recommended for premium brand)
